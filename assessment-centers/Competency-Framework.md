# Competency Framework for Talent Development

## Core Competencies

### 1. Leadership & Strategic Thinking (Weight: 25%)
- **Proficiency Levels:** 1-5
- **Definition:** Ability to envision future state and guide organizational change
- **Behavioral Indicators:**
  - Develops strategic plans
  - Communicates clear vision
  - Makes informed decisions

### 2. People Development (Weight: 25%)
- **Proficiency Levels:** 1-5
- **Definition:** Ability to identify talent and create growth opportunities
- **Behavioral Indicators:**
  - Creates development plans
  - Provides constructive feedback
  - Mentors and coaches teams

### 3. Communication & Influence (Weight: 20%)
- **Proficiency Levels:** 1-5
- **Definition:** Ability to convey ideas effectively and influence others
- **Behavioral Indicators:**
  - Clear, concise communication
  - Builds rapport
  - Persuades stakeholders

### 4. Analytical & Problem Solving (Weight: 15%)
- **Proficiency Levels:** 1-5
- **Definition:** Ability to analyze data and create innovative solutions
- **Behavioral Indicators:**
  - Gathers comprehensive data
  - Identifies root causes
  - Implements sustainable solutions

### 5. Collaboration & Teamwork (Weight: 15%)
- **Proficiency Levels:** 1-5
- **Definition:** Ability to work effectively with diverse teams
- **Behavioral Indicators:**
  - Shares information openly
  - Supports team goals
  - Resolves conflicts constructively

## Evaluation Scale

- **Level 1:** Below Expectations - Does not demonstrate competency
- **Level 2:** Developing - Inconsistent demonstration of competency
- **Level 3:** Meets Expectations - Consistent demonstration of competency
- **Level 4:** Exceeds Expectations - Strong demonstration of competency
- **Level 5:** Distinguished - Exemplary demonstration of competency
