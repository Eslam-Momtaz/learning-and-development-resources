# Assessment Center Design Guide

## Overview
This guide provides a comprehensive framework for designing and conducting assessment centers for talent evaluation and development.

## Key Components

### 1. Competency Framework
- Identify core competencies required for the role
- Define behavioral indicators for each competency
- Establish proficiency levels (1-5 scale)
- Align with organizational strategy

### 2. Assessment Methods
- In-basket exercises
- Group discussions and presentations
- One-on-one interviews
- Psychometric assessments
- Role-plays and simulations
- Case studies and problem-solving tasks

### 3. Facilitation Best Practices
- Train and calibrate assessors thoroughly
- Ensure standardized evaluation across all participants
- Maintain detailed observation notes
- Document behavioral evidence for each competency
- Use structured scoring rubrics

### 4. Feedback & Development
- Provide comprehensive feedback reports
- Identify strengths and development areas
- Create individualized development plans
- Follow-up coaching and support

## Implementation Timeline
- Week 1-2: Competency framework development
- Week 3-4: Assessor training and calibration
- Week 5-8: Assessment execution
- Week 9-10: Feedback and reporting

## Success Metrics
- Participant satisfaction scores
- Assessment reliability (inter-rater agreement)
- Correlation with job performance
- Development plan completion rates
